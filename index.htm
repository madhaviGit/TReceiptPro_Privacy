Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the TReceipt Pro app ("Application"), developed by Madhavi Gullipalli ("Service Provider").

Effective Date: 2026-04-10

Information Collection

The Application may collect the following information:

  • Device information (e.g. operating system, device type)
  • Usage data (e.g. app interactions, time spent)
  • IP address (may be collected indirectly by Firebase services for security and analytics purposes)
  • Name and email (via Google Sign-In, if used)
  • Receipt images and data that you choose to scan or upload

Device Permissions

The Application requests the following device permissions to provide core functionality:

  • Camera: Used to scan and photograph receipts directly within the app.
  • Photo Library / Gallery: Used to allow you to select existing receipt images from your device.
  • Storage: Used to save exported Excel files to your device. Receipt data is stored securely in the cloud (Firebase) and is not stored locally on your device.

These permissions are used solely to provide the receipt management features of the Application. Receipt images are transmitted to Google Gemini AI for automated data extraction. No other images or files are shared with third parties without your explicit action.

Use of Data

Data is used to:

  • Provide and improve the Application
  • Monitor performance and fix issues
  • Authenticate users
  • Process and display your scanned receipts

In-App Purchases

TReceipt Pro uses a pay-as-you-go model via In-App Purchases. Users purchase scan packs (a fixed number of receipt scans) as one-time in-app purchases through the Google Play Store (Android) or Apple App Store (iOS).

  • The Service Provider does not collect, process, or store any payment or billing information.
  • All purchases are handled by Google Play Billing or Apple In-App Purchases.
  • Scan credits are non-refundable once used.
  • Payment details are governed by Google's and Apple's respective privacy policies.

Third-Party Services

The Application uses the following third-party services, which may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics (crash reporting)
  • Firebase Authentication
  • Google Sign-In
  • Google Gemini AI (receipt content processing)

AI Processing

Receipt images and data are processed by Google Gemini AI to extract information such as merchant name, date, total amount, and line items. Images sent to Gemini are used solely for extraction purposes and are not stored or used for AI training by the Service Provider. Processing is subject to Google's Gemini API Terms and Privacy Policy.

Crash Reporting

The Application uses Firebase Crashlytics to automatically collect crash reports. These reports may include device state, app version, and stack traces at the time of a crash. No personally identifiable information is intentionally included in crash reports. This data is used solely to identify and fix bugs.

Data Retention

Data is retained while you use the Application and for up to 30 days after account deletion or app uninstallation, unless a longer retention period is required by law.
